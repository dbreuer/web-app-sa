Troubleshooting guide

If you can't access your MyAAT account, reset your password - instructions and a link will be sent to the email address you registered with.

Contact us

If you still can't access your account, please contact our team for help. Please have your membership number ready.

Tel: +44 (0)20 3735 2468
Email: membershipsupport@aat.org.uk. Use the email address you use to login to MyAAT or we will ask you to confirm your identity.

Our telephone lines are open Monday to Friday, 09.00 to 17.00 UK time.
